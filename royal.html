  • Royal Enfield is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing company headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The company is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production and operates manufacturing plants in Chennai in India.
  • The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in 1901 by The Enfield Cycle Company of Redditch, Worcestershire, England.
  • Royal Enfield is a global brand, with a presence in over 60 countries and a strong following among motorcycle enthusiasts.
  • Royal Enfield motorcycles are known for their classic styling, rugged build quality, and distinctive thumping sound.
  • The company offers a range of motorcycles, including classic models like the Bullet and modern classics like the Interceptor and Continental GT.
